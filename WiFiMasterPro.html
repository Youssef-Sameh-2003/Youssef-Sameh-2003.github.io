About WiFi Master Pro

WiFi Master Pro is an advanced WiFi security app designed to detect and manage devices connected to your WiFi network. Ensure the safety and integrity of your network with powerful features.

Key Features

Device Detection

Identify all devices connected to your WiFi network, including unauthorized or unknown devices.

Real-time Monitoring

Monitor your WiFi network in real-time and receive instant notifications about new or suspicious devices.

Device Information

Get detailed information about each connected device, including device name, IP address, and MAC address.

Secure Your Network

Take action against unwanted devices by blocking or restricting their access to your WiFi network.

Network Health Check

Ensure the health and performance of your WiFi network with WiFi Master Pro's diagnostic tools.

User-Friendly Interface
